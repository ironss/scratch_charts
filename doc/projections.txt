Common projections
==================

EPSG:3395    WGS 84 / World Mercator                           Very small scale mappings.
EPSG:4326    WGS 84                                            Horizontal component of 3D system. Used by GPS.
EPSG:2193    NZGD2000 / New Zealand Transverse Mercator 2000   Large and medium scale topographic mapping and engineering survey.
EPSG:27200   NZGD49 / New Zealand Map Grid                     Large and medium scale topographic mapping and engineering survey.

Old projections
===============
EPSG:3857    Both of these were earlier 'World Mercator' types, and have been
EPSG:900913  superceded by EPSG:3395
